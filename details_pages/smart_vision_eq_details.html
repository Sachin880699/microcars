Smart Vision EQ

The Smart Vision EQ Fortwo is a futuristic fully electric and autonomous concept city car developed by Smart (Daimler AG). First unveiled at the 2017 Frankfurt Motor Show (IAA), this concept highlights Smart’s vision for next-generation urban mobility, focusing on zero-emission driving, autonomous technology, and shared transportation solutions for modern cities.

Compact Design: Optimized for Urban Environments

At the core of the Smart Vision EQ is a compact and lightweight vehicle platform designed specifically for dense urban areas. Its small footprint allows easy maneuverability through narrow streets and tight parking spaces. The design emphasizes efficiency, visibility, and pedestrian-friendly interaction, making it ideal for future smart cities.

Fully Electric Autonomous Platform

The Smart Vision EQ operates as a fully autonomous electric vehicle built for city use. It eliminates traditional driving controls such as a steering wheel and pedals, relying entirely on advanced sensors, cameras, and artificial intelligence systems. This enables safe, smooth navigation through city traffic while producing zero tailpipe emissions.

Smart Interaction and Communication

A standout feature of the Smart Vision EQ is its external LED communication system, which displays messages and symbols to pedestrians and other road users. This system enhances safety by clearly indicating the vehicle’s intentions, such as stopping, turning, or yielding, improving trust between autonomous vehicles and people.

Interior and Passenger Experience

The interior of the Smart Vision EQ follows a minimalist and lounge-like design philosophy. With no manual controls, the cabin focuses entirely on passenger comfort, digital connectivity, and entertainment. Large displays provide journey information, navigation updates, and personalized content, creating a relaxed urban travel experience.

Vision for Future Smart Cities

Although the Smart Vision EQ is a concept vehicle and not intended for production, it represents an important step toward sustainable and shared urban mobility. Designed primarily for car-sharing and mobility services, it demonstrates how autonomous electric vehicles can reduce congestion, emissions, and private car ownership in cities.

The Smart Vision EQ serves as a blueprint for future autonomous electric city cars, showcasing how intelligent design, connectivity, and sustainability can shape the future of urban transportation.
