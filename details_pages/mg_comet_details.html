MG Comet EV

The MG Comet EV is a compact, affordable electric city car designed for short urban trips and easy maneuverability. With a "big inside, compact outside" philosophy, the Comet offers practical interior space and simple, efficient electric propulsion tailored to everyday commuting.

The Comet focuses on low-speed urban efficiency rather than high performance. Its lightweight construction and electric drivetrain deliver brisk low-end torque, low running costs, and a small footprint that makes parking and navigating tight streets effortless.

Battery & Range

Depending on configuration, the Comet uses a compact battery pack suitable for city use with an estimated real-world range designed to cover daily commutes. Charging is supported through standard AC outlets and public chargers; some versions may support faster charging for quicker top-ups.

Practical Urban Features

The cabin is efficiently packaged to seat two to four occupants (depending on layout) and provide a modest cargo area. Features typically include basic infotainment with smartphone connectivity, parking assistance, and safety features appropriate for urban driving scenarios.

Connectivity & Safety

MG equips the Comet with essential connectivity and safety technology such as a touchscreen infotainment, reversing camera, electronic stability aids, and multiple airbags, focusing on value and usability for urban drivers.

Here are the estimated technical specifications for the MG Comet EV. Values shown are typical for city-oriented micro-EVs and may vary by market and trim.

Component Specification
Body Type Compact city hatchback / microcar
Seating Capacity 2–4 (depending on configuration)
Overall Length ~2.6–2.9 meters
