Audi Pop.Up Next

The Audi Pop.Up Next is a futuristic electric and autonomous flying car concept developed by Audi in collaboration with Airbus and Italdesign. First unveiled at the 2018 Geneva Motor Show, this concept showcases Audi’s vision for next-generation urban mobility, combining road travel and air transport into a single modular system designed to reduce traffic congestion and emissions in modern cities.

Modular Design: One Passenger Pod, Two Transport Modes

At the heart of the Audi Pop.Up Next is a lightweight passenger capsule built using advanced materials such as carbon fiber. This capsule is designed to dock with two different modules depending on travel requirements. For city driving, it connects to a fully electric ground module, while for longer or congested routes, it can be lifted and attached to an autonomous aerial module capable of vertical take-off and landing (VTOL).

Electric Autonomous Ground Module

In road mode, the Pop.Up Next operates as a self-driving electric vehicle optimized for urban environments. The ground module features in-wheel electric motors, a compact chassis, and zero tailpipe emissions. Advanced autonomous driving systems allow the vehicle to navigate city traffic safely while integrating with smart-city infrastructure and mobility networks.

Flying Vehicle Technology by Airbus

The aerial module, developed with expertise from Airbus, transforms the Pop.Up Next into a fully electric flying vehicle. Using multiple electric rotors, it enables vertical take-off and landing, similar to a drone. The system is designed for autonomous operation, following predefined air routes and safety protocols to ensure efficient and secure urban air mobility.

Interior and Passenger Experience

The interior of the Audi Pop.Up Next reflects a minimalist and futuristic design philosophy. As a fully autonomous vehicle, it eliminates traditional driving controls and instead focuses on passenger comfort, digital interfaces, and smart connectivity. Large displays provide journey information, route planning, and real-time mobility updates, creating a lounge-like travel experience.

Vision for Future Smart Cities

Although the Audi Pop.Up Next is a concept vehicle and not in production, it represents an important step toward sustainable urban transportation. By combining electric power, autonomous technology, and modular mobility, Audi demonstrates how future cities could reduce congestion, lower emissions, and offer faster, more flexible transportation options.

The Pop.Up Next serves as a blueprint for future electric flying cars, highlighting how automotive and aerospace innovation can merge to shape the future of mobility.
