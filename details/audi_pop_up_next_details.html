Smart Vision EQ

Smart Vision EQ is a fully autonomous electric concept car designed specifically for dense urban environments. It focuses on shared mobility, zero emissions, and intelligent transportation systems.

The vehicle does not include a traditional steering wheel or pedals. Instead, it relies on advanced sensors and AI-based navigation to move passengers safely through city streets.

Key Specifications

  • Vehicle Type: Autonomous Electric Concept
  • Seating Capacity: 2 Passengers
  • Drive System: Fully Electric
  • Autonomy Level: Self-driving (Concept)
  • Primary Use: Urban shared mobility
  • Design Goal: Space efficiency & sustainability
