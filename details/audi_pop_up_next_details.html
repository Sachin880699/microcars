Audi Pop.Up Next

Audi Pop.Up Next is a futuristic urban mobility concept developed by Audi in collaboration with Airbus and Italdesign. It is designed to address traffic congestion in large cities by combining road-based electric driving with vertical take-off and landing (VTOL) air travel.

The concept uses a modular approach where a lightweight passenger capsule can connect to either a self-driving electric ground module or a drone-based flight module. This allows passengers to seamlessly switch between driving and flying without leaving the capsule.

Audi Pop.Up Next is not intended for mass production but serves as a vision for future autonomous, electric, and shared transportation systems in smart cities.

Key Specifications

  • Vehicle Type: Modular Electric & Flying Concept
  • Manufacturer: Audi (with Airbus & Italdesign)
  • Seating Capacity: 2 Passengers
  • Drive System: Fully Electric
  • Ground Module: Autonomous electric platform
  • Air Module: VTOL drone with multiple rotors
  • Autonomy Level: Fully self-driving (Concept)
  • Primary Use: Urban & inter-city mobility
  • Energy Source: Battery-powered (electric)
  • Design Focus: Traffic reduction & smart mobility
  • First Revealed: Geneva Motor Show 2018
